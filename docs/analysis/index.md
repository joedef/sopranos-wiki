## A note on method

**Interpretation is labeled.** Where a reading is contested, the page says who contests it. Chase's stated intentions are given as *his stated intentions*, not as the meaning — an author's account of their own work is evidence, not a verdict.

**The ending stays open.** [Reading the Ending](reading-the-ending.md) presents the major interpretations without declaring a winner, and no other page asserts that Tony definitely lives or dies. That's a deliberate editorial position, not an oversight. Chase's own statements have moved across eighteen years; the wiki tracks them rather than resolving them.

**The show is allowed to contradict itself.** *The Sopranos* is funny about atrocities and serious about a duck. Any reading that makes it fully coherent has flattened it.

Counter-readings are the most welcome contribution here. See [Contributing](https://github.com/joedef/sopranos-wiki/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
