## A note on the DiMeo family

The fictional organization is the **DiMeo crime family** — occasionally called the Soprano family by outsiders and the FBI, which irritates its members.

Its structure follows the real thing: a boss, an underboss, a consigliere, capos running crews, soldiers, and associates. Ercole "Eckley" DiMeo is the imprisoned official boss for most of the series; the operational authority runs Jackie Aprile Sr. → [Junior](junior-soprano.md) (nominally) → [Tony](tony-soprano.md).

It's modeled substantially on the real **[DeCavalcante family](../reference/real-world-mafia.md)** of North Jersey — the "sixth family," perpetually overshadowed by the New York Five, which is exactly the status anxiety the show runs on.
