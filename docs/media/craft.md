**Final scene analysis.** A shot-by-shot walk through the last four and a half minutes at Holsten's — the point-of-view cutting pattern, the door chimes, and the reverse shot that never arrives. Whatever you conclude, this is the **editing grammar** any reading has to account for. See [Reading the Ending](../analysis/reading-the-ending.md).

## What isn't here

Three things that beat any video essay, and can't be embedded:

***Wise Guy: David Chase and The Sopranos*** **(2024)** — Alex Gibney's two-part HBO documentary. The most substantial on-the-record account from Chase that exists. Its trailer is on [Scenes & Trailers](scenes.md).

***Talking Sopranos*** — [Michael Imperioli](../people/michael-imperioli.md) and Steve Schirripa going through all 86 episodes with the people who made them. More first-hand production detail than any book.

***The Sopranos Sessions*** **(2019)** — Alan Sepinwall and Matt Zoller Seitz's episode-by-episode book, which includes the long Chase interviews containing the "that death scene" exchange. See [Sources](../reference/sources.md).
