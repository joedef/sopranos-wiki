**Season 1 opening credits.** Ninety seconds that contain the whole show: [Tony](../characters/tony-soprano.md) emerging from the Lincoln Tunnel, taking a ticket, and driving the Turnpike past Newark, the refineries, and the cemeteries out to a lawn in North Caldwell. The immigrant story compressed into a commute, scored to a British acid-house-blues track. **Watch the side mirror** — the World Trade Center appears here and was removed after 2001. — *HBO Classics*

**The first therapy session.** Tony in [Melfi's](../characters/dr-melfi.md) waiting room, under the statue, and then the line the whole series is built on: that he came in at the end, and the best is over. Everything the show is about is stated in the first ten minutes and then demonstrated for eighty-six hours. See [Themes](../analysis/themes.md). — *HBO*

## Where to find more

Rather than embed dozens of clips that will break:

- **[HBO](https://www.youtube.com/@hbo)** and **HBO Classics** post and periodically refresh authorized clips.
- **The Blu-ray and streaming supplements** carry commentaries and behind-the-scenes material that is not reliably on YouTube.
- ***Talking Sopranos*** — [Michael Imperioli](../people/michael-imperioli.md) and Steve Schirripa's episode-by-episode rewatch podcast, which contains more first-hand production detail than any other single source.
