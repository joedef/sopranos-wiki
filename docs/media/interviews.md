{ .video-caption }

!!! warning "On 'Chase explains the ending' videos"
    A large number of YouTube uploads claim Chase confirmed Tony's death — mostly downstream of a **November 2021 *Hollywood Reporter* piece** that was widely misreported. Chase's own response to that coverage was: *"Everybody who believes I said Tony is dead in a Hollywood Reporter article: works for me."* He clarified again in 2024 that the scene implies Tony **could** die, not that he does.

    Treat any video titled "Chase finally reveals" with suspicion, and check it against the dated chronology on [The Ending](../the-ending.md).

**The cost of playing Tony.** On what the role took out of him — the sleep deprivation, the stone in the shoe, the disappearances, the apologies afterward. [Gandolfini](../people/james-gandolfini.md) was, by every account, nothing like the character and found inhabiting him genuinely difficult.

**Gandolfini on Tony's anger.** Him talking about where the rage came from and how he reached it. Notable mostly for how uncomfortable he is discussing his own work — a shy man being asked to explain the most frightening performance on television.
