## The Goodfellas overlap

**Twenty-seven actors** appear in both Martin Scorsese's 1990 film and this series — three of them as *Sopranos* regulars.

The most significant: **[Lorraine Bracco](lorraine-bracco.md)** (Karen Hill → Dr. Melfi), **[Michael Imperioli](michael-imperioli.md)** (Spider, who gets shot in the foot → Christopher, who shoots someone in the foot), **[Tony Sirico](tony-sirico.md)**, **[Vincent Pastore](vincent-pastore.md)**, **Frank Vincent** (Billy Batts → Phil Leotardo), and **Frank Sivero**.

It isn't a coincidence or a budget decision. Casting directors Georgianne Walken and Sheila Jaffe were drawing from the same New York/New Jersey pool, and Chase was deliberately building on the audience's memory of those faces. See [Mob Movies & Self-Awareness](../analysis/mob-movies-and-self-awareness.md).
