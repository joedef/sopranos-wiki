# Supporting Cast

The character actors who made New Jersey feel populated.

---

## The crew

**Steve Schirripa** — [Bobby Baccalieri](../characters/bobby-baccalieri.md). A former **Las Vegas casino executive** (entertainment director at the Riviera) with no acting background. Since 2020 he has co-hosted ***Talking Sopranos*** with [Michael Imperioli](michael-imperioli.md), one of the main engines of the show's streaming revival.

**Vincent Curatola** — [Johnny Sack](../characters/johnny-sack.md). A businessman with no formal training who got the audition by chance. His perp-walk scene in "Mr. & Mrs. John Sacrimoni Request…" is one of the show's most uncomfortable, and he has said it was the hardest thing he was asked to do.

**Frank Vincent** — [Phil Leotardo](../characters/phil-leotardo.md). Genre royalty: **Billy Batts in *Goodfellas*** (*go home and get your shining box*), plus *Raging Bull* and *Casino*. Died September 13, 2017, aged 80.

**David Proval** — [Richie Aprile](../characters/richie-aprile.md). Best known before this for Scorsese's ***Mean Streets*** (1973).

**Joseph R. Gannascoli** — [Vito Spatafore](../characters/vito-spatafore.md). A former chef and restaurateur who reportedly pitched the character's storyline himself after reading a book about a gay mobster.

**Federico Castelluccio** — [Furio Giunta](../characters/furio-giunta.md). Born in Naples, raised in Paterson, NJ. Also an accomplished painter, known in the art world for identifying a lost Baroque work.

**Jerry Adler** — Hesh Rabkin. A **Broadway stage manager and director for decades** before turning to acting in his sixties. Also *The Good Wife* and *Rescue Me*.

**Dan Grimaldi** — Patsy Parisi (and his twin Philly). Also a **mathematics professor** at Kingsborough Community College, teaching throughout the show's run.

**Max Casella** — Benny Fazio. Previously Vinnie Delpino on *Doogie Howser, M.D.*, and later *Boardwalk Empire* and *Vinyl*.

**Robert Funaro** — Eugene Pontecorvo. **Michael Rispoli** — Jackie Aprile Sr. **Jason Cerbone** — Jackie Jr. **Al Sapienza** — Mikey Palmice. **Arthur Nascarella** — Carlo Gervasi. **John Ventimiglia** — [Artie Bucco](../characters/artie-bucco.md).

## The women

**Kathrine Narducci** — Charmaine Bucco. Also *A Bronx Tale* and *The Irishman*.

**Sharon Angela** — Rosalie Aprile. **Annabella Sciorra** — Gloria Trillo, in a season-three arc that earned an Emmy nomination. **Alla Kliouka** — Svetlana Kirilenko. **Lola Glaudini** — Agent Deborah Ciccerone, later a *Criminal Minds* lead. **Denise Borino-Quinn** — Ginny Sacrimoni, cast from an open call and not a professional actress; died in 2010, aged 46.

---

## The professionals

**Matt Servitto** — Agent Dwight Harris, across all six seasons. **Peter Bogdanovich** — Dr. Elliot Kupferberg, and director of "Sentimental Education." **Paul Schulze** — Father Phil. **David Strathairn** — Robert Wegler. **Robert Patrick** — Davey Scatino. **Tony Lip** — Carmine Lupertazzi Sr.; a real former bouncer and driver, and the subject of the film *Green Book*. **Ray Abruzzo** — Little Carmine. **Greg Antonacci** — Butch DeConcini.

## The Goodfellas overlap

**Twenty-seven actors** appear in both Scorsese's 1990 film and this series.

The significant ones: **[Lorraine Bracco](lorraine-bracco.md)** (Karen Hill → Melfi), **[Michael Imperioli](michael-imperioli.md)** (Spider → Christopher), **[Tony Sirico](tony-sirico.md)**, **[Vincent Pastore](vincent-pastore.md)**, **Frank Vincent** (Billy Batts → Phil), **Frank Sivero**, **Chuck Low**, and **Suzanne Shepherd** (Karen's mother → Carmela's mother, Mary DeAngelis — playing essentially the same function twice).

Casting directors **Georgianne Walken** and **Sheila Jaffe** were drawing from the same New York/New Jersey talent pool, and Chase was deliberately trading on the audience's memory of those faces. It's not economy; it's an argument. See [Mob Movies & Self-Awareness](../analysis/mob-movies-and-self-awareness.md).
