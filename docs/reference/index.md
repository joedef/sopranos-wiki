## A standing note on reliability

*Sopranos* production history is in better shape than most, because the participants are still alive, still talking, and largely consistent — the *Talking Sopranos* podcast alone has generated hundreds of hours of first-hand recollection.

Two cautions all the same:

**Twenty-five years of anecdote smooths.** Convention panels and podcasts reward a good story, and several of the most-repeated ones have acquired detail over time. Where accounts conflict, this wiki says so.

**Chase's statements about the ending are a moving target.** He has said different things in 2007, 2015, 2019, 2021, and 2024. Any single quotation presented as final is misleading by omission. [The Ending](../the-ending.md) gives them all, dated, which is the only honest way to handle it.
