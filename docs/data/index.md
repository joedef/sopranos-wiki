## Caveats

**Viewership figures** are Nielsen numbers for the original HBO airing. HBO reported multiple metrics — premiere airing, same-night repeats, weekly cumulative — and different sources use different ones. Figures here are premiere-airing averages unless stated.

**Body counts** vary by what you count. This wiki counts named characters whose deaths are shown or explicitly reported. Off-screen and implied deaths are marked.

**In-universe dates** are reconstructed. The show is deliberately loose about ages and elapsed time, and there are known internal inconsistencies — most famously around [Tony's](../characters/tony-soprano.md) and [Janice's](../characters/janice-soprano.md) birth years.
