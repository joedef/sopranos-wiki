# Timeline

The story runs roughly in real time — each season is set at about the moment it aired, which is unusual and is why the show registers the era so precisely.

---

## Known inconsistencies

The show is loose about dates, and a reference wiki should say so.

**Tony's age** is inconsistent across seasons — dialogue at various points implies birth years from roughly 1957 to 1960.

**Janice's age** relative to Tony shifts.

**Christopher's relationship** to Tony is described as "nephew" throughout but is actually cousin-by-marriage — [Carmela's](../characters/carmela-soprano.md) first cousin. See [Family Tree](../characters/family-tree.md).

**The gap between seasons 5 and 6** is about two years in the real world and considerably less in-universe, which produces some strain around A.J.'s and Meadow's ages.

None of this was ever tidied, and Chase has never shown any interest in doing so.
