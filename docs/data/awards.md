# Awards

**21 Primetime Emmy wins from 111 nominations**, and the show that ended network television's monopoly on the drama category.

---

## The breakthrough

In **2004**, *The Sopranos* became the **first series on a cable network ever to win Outstanding Drama Series.**

Before that, the category had been a network preserve for its entire existence. After it, cable and then streaming took it almost permanently. It is the single most consequential award in the show's record and arguably in the medium's history.

It won again in **2007** for the final season.

## The notable losses

**[Lorraine Bracco](../people/lorraine-bracco.md)** — four nominations for [Dr. Melfi](../characters/dr-melfi.md), **no wins**. The most conspicuous omission in the record.

**[Tony Sirico](../people/tony-sirico.md)** — **never nominated**, for [Paulie](../characters/paulie-walnuts.md).

**[Steven Van Zandt](../people/steven-van-zandt.md)** — never nominated.

**[Dominic Chianese](../people/dominic-chianese.md)** — one nomination across six seasons.

**[Aida Turturro](../people/aida-turturro.md)** — two nominations, no wins.

**Steve Buscemi** — nominated for [Tony Blundetto](../characters/tony-blundetto.md), no win.

And the show lost Outstanding Drama Series in **1999, 2000, 2001, 2003, and 2006** — five defeats before its first win, mostly to *The West Wing*, which is now the standard example of Emmy voters preferring the reassuring option. It was nominated **seven times in total, in every year it was eligible**; the long hiatuses between seasons left it out of contention entirely in some years, including 2002.

## Rankings

- ***Rolling Stone***, *Time*, *Entertainment Weekly*, *Variety*, and the *Writers Guild of America* have all placed it **#1** on greatest-television-series lists.
- The **WGA's 101 Best-Written TV Series** (2013) ranked it **#1**.
- ***Empire*** and the **BBC** have ranked it among the greatest television ever made.
- **"Pine Barrens"** and **"College"** appear near the top of essentially every greatest-episodes list. *TV Guide* placed "College" **#2** on its 100 Greatest Episodes of All Time.
