# Support the Project

This wiki is free, open-source, ad-light, and maintained by volunteers. It costs almost nothing to run — GitHub Pages hosting is free — so the most valuable support isn't money.

---

## The best support is free

In rough order of usefulness:

- **Fix something.** Corrections are the single most valuable contribution. Every page has an ✏️ edit link, and [Contributing](https://github.com/joedef/sopranos-wiki/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) explains the (short) rules.
- **⭐ Star the [repository](https://github.com/joedef/sopranos-wiki).** It costs you nothing and it's how other people find the project.
- **Share a page** you found useful. A link in a Reddit thread or a Discord does more for a niche wiki than any amount of SEO.
- **Report a dead video embed** or a broken link by [opening an issue](https://github.com/joedef/sopranos-wiki/issues).

    Links in this section are affiliate links. As an Amazon Associate the project may earn from qualifying purchases. It doesn't change the price you pay, and it doesn't change what this wiki says — the [recommendations](reference/sources.md) predate the links and are made on the merits.

- [The Many Saints of Newark](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=the+many+saints+of+newark+blu+ray) — the 2021 prequel. See [the film](series/the-many-saints-of-newark.md).

## What your support does *not* buy

Editorial influence. This is an independent fan project with no relationship to HBO, Warner Bros. Discovery, David Chase, or anyone who profits from *The Sopranos*. Affiliate commissions and contributions cover incidental costs and the maintainer's time; they don't decide what the pages say. If a page ever reads like an advertisement, [tell us](https://github.com/joedef/sopranos-wiki/issues) — that's a bug.
